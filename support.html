Frequently asked questions

Where are my screenshots stored?

ClipShot stores them locally on your Mac, at ~/Library/Containers/com.jcmorla.clipshot/Data/Library/Application Support/ClipShot/history/, organized in monthly subfolders. Choose Open history folder in the menu to view them.

Does ClipShot upload my screenshots anywhere?

Never. ClipShot has no networking code, no analytics, and no cloud sync. Everything stays on your Mac.

How do I take a screenshot?

Use the standard macOS shortcuts you already know — Cmd+Shift+3, 4, or 5. ClipShot picks up your captures automatically.

How do I clear my history?

Click the ClipShot icon in the menu bar, then Clear history…. You can delete everything or just items older than 30 days.

What languages does ClipShot support?

English, Spanish, and French. The app picks the language automatically based on your macOS preferences, with English as the fallback.

How do I change the screenshot folder?

From the menu, choose Preferences → Change screenshot folder… and pick any folder you like.

Does ClipShot start automatically with my Mac?

Only if you enabled Open at login during the welcome flow or in Preferences. Otherwise it stays off.

How do I uninstall ClipShot?

Quit ClipShot from the menu, then drag the app from Applications to the Trash. To remove all data, also delete ~/Library/Containers/com.jcmorla.clipshot/.
