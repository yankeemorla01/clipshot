## 🇬🇧 English

**ClipShot** is a **free** Mac app that quietly lives in your menu bar and saves every screenshot you take, automatically.

No more hunting through Desktop, Downloads, or that one folder you swore you'd organize. Open the menu, see your full history grouped by month, and click any thumbnail to re-copy it to the clipboard.

### Why you'll love it

- 📸 **Every screenshot, saved** — captures appear in your history within seconds
- 📋 **Re-copy with one click** — past screenshots flow straight back to the clipboard
- 🖼️ **Floating thumbnail** — click to edit in Markup, or drag straight into any app
- 📅 **Organized by month** — folders like *January 2026* keep everything findable
- 🌍 **Available in 3 languages** — English, Spanish, French (auto-detects from macOS)

### 🔒 Privacy, by design

ClipShot runs **100% on your Mac**. No accounts. No servers. No telemetry. Nothing is ever sent to the internet — not a single byte. Your screenshots are yours, and only yours.

### Get it

**Free on the Mac App Store.** Just download, install, and take a screenshot — it's already saved.

[Download on the Mac App Store →](https://apps.apple.com/)
